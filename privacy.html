PRIVACY

last updated: July 2026

Hold collects nothing. There is no data to disclose, sell, or leak, because none ever leaves your device:

  • No data collection. No analytics, no crash reporting, no identifiers, no advertising SDKs.
  • No network access. The app is fully offline. The daily challenge is computed on your device from the calendar date.
  • No accounts. Nothing to sign up for, nothing to log in to.
  • Your scores stay yours. Results, streaks, and settings are stored only on your device and are deleted when you delete the app. Sharing a result happens through the standard iOS share sheet, entirely under your control.

If Hold ever adds a feature that touches the network, this policy will be updated before that version ships.
